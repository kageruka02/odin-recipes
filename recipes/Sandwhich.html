Larry-David-Sandwhich

Description

The "Larry David Sandwich" is a quirky yet delicious combination of flavors and textures, paying homage to the comedic genius's unique persona and culinary preferences. With its bold New York-inspired ingredients and contrasting elements, it's a sandwich that's sure to tickle your taste buds and evoke a smile, much like Larry David himself

So, whether you're enjoying it for breakfast, lunch, or a late-night snack, savor each bite and embrace the spirit of spontaneity and humor embodied by Larry David and his legendary character.

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cream Cheese
  • Capers
  • Red Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Dill Pickle

Steps

  1. Toast the Bagel
  2. Layer Ingredients
  3. Close and Serve
