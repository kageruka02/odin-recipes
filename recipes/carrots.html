carrots

Description

To prepare delicious glazed carrots, start by peeling and slicing fresh carrots into uniform pieces. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and add the sliced carrots, along with a pinch of salt and a sprinkle of brown sugar for sweetness. the carrots until they start to soften, then pour in enough chicken or vegetable broth to cover them halfway. Bring the broth to a simmer and let the carrots cook until tender, stirring occasionally.

Once the carrots are cooked through and the liquid has reduced to a glaze, remove them from the heat. Finish the dish by garnishing with freshly chopped parsley or thyme for a burst of flavor and color. These glazed carrots make a perfect side dish for any meal, adding a touch of sweetness and brightness to the table.
