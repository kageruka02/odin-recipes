pineapple

Description

Scalloped Pineapple Soufflé is a harmonious blend of sweet pineapple, creamy texture, and delicate soufflé airiness. With each spoonful, you're greeted by the vibrant tropical flavor of crushed pineapple, which infuses every bite with a refreshing sweetness. The soufflé's texture is ethereal and light, thanks to the incorporation of whipped egg whites, which lend a fluffy consistency that practically melts in your mouth.

As you savor each mouthful, the subtle hints of vanilla and butter add depth to the dish, enhancing its overall richness and aroma. It's a delightful balance of flavors and textures that tantalizes the taste buds and leaves you craving another bite.

Ingredients

  • crushed pineapple
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 3 tablespoons
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Prepare Pineapple Mixture
  3. Add Egg Yolks
  4. Add Flour and Flavoring
  5. Beat Egg Whites
  6. Fold Egg whites
  7. Bake
  8. Serve
